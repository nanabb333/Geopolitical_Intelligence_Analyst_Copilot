# V2 Pilot Event Selection

## Selection Objective

This document explains why seven V2 pilot events were selected for future market-mapping review.

The selected events are planning candidates only. No market data has been populated, no returns have been calculated, and no investment conclusions should be inferred.

## Selected Events

| Event ID | Event Name | Category |
| --- | --- | --- |
| V2P001 | CHIPS and Science Act signed | Industrial Policy |
| V2P002 | BIS advanced computing and semiconductor manufacturing export controls | Export Controls |
| V2P003 | YMTC and other Chinese entities added to Entity List | Sanctions / Entity List |
| V2P006 | China announces gallium and germanium export controls | Critical Minerals |
| V2P007 | US executive order on outbound investment in sensitive technologies | Strategic Investment Screening |
| V2P008 | BIS updates advanced computing and semiconductor export controls | AI Chip Restrictions |
| V2P011 | TSMC Arizona CHIPS preliminary terms announced | Supply Chain Relocation |

## Why These Events Were Selected

These events were selected because they:

- cover the main V2 pilot categories;
- are adjacent to the current semiconductor-centered V1 dataset;
- have clear event dates and source documentation;
- have plausible asset, sector, or benchmark proxies for future review;
- represent different market-mapping challenges;
- do not require the project to invent market reactions.

## Categories Covered

Covered categories:

- Export Controls
- Sanctions / Entity List
- AI Chip Restrictions
- Industrial Policy
- Strategic Investment Screening
- Critical Minerals
- Supply Chain Relocation

## Categories Remaining Uncovered

Not covered in this first market-mapping selection:

- Taiwan-related tension
- China-side industrial policy
- Graphite or rare-earth critical-minerals events beyond gallium/germanium
- Later outbound-investment final rule implementation
- Later HBM and semiconductor-manufacturing control update

These are not excluded permanently. They are deferred because the first market-mapping batch should prioritize clearer event-to-asset mapping and avoid broad risk-sentiment proxies before rules are mature.

## Why This Is a Pilot Rather Than a Full Market Dataset

This is a pilot because:

- only seven events are selected;
- no return rows have been populated;
- asset and benchmark proxies are preliminary;
- event-date and trading-calendar conventions still require review;
- market data sources have not been chosen;
- no V2 rows are integrated into the workbench.

The purpose is to prepare market-data collection, not to claim market-reaction coverage.

## Limitations

- Some events are broad policy announcements and may be highly anticipated.
- Some event effects are indirect and may require sector proxies rather than named assets.
- Some named companies may lack clean public-equity mapping.
- Benchmark choice can materially change abnormal-return interpretation.
- Confounds such as earnings, macro news, and concurrent policy events must be reviewed before any return row is accepted.
